Our Story

What Makes 365sol different?

We began with the desire to help the world ----

What we knew was travel to your passport to amazing places

When I was 10, I was watching TV by myself one morning. The show was paying tribute to the people who had passed that year and many contributions they made to make the world a better place. I was mesmerized. I thought I did not want to leave this world without doing anything without making a difference. My quest had started.

As I grew up and older, I believed business should do more than just make the company money. That making a difference in people’s lives should be the result of a good business well run.

We believe the best run businesses improve the lives of everyone it touches, the customers, the staff and their families and mankind. We also believed business should offer benefits to all people in and around the business- Lisa Bettner, CEO

We believe life is about the stories, you have to tell, the things you have accomplished and the people you help.

We donate 20% of profits to charity. see more

So we built 365sol to be a trusted source to traveler, an indispensable service to the listing travel businesses, and a force in making the world better when we leave it then when we found it.
